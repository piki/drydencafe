Become a Member

While the Dryden Community Center Café is open to everyone regardless of membership, we encourage our friends and neighbors to be a part of a non-profit organization committed to having a positive impact on the quality of life in our community!

Become a member! Your membership dollars will be used for the improvement of our building, securing equipment and supplies for our educational programs, and continued operational support. Your membership will allow you to vote at meetings and is a great way to let your thoughts be heard. Memberships are $10 per person.

Please fill out the membership form, or just let us know your name, address, and/or email address.

Thank you so much joining us in this wonderful project!

Please make your check made payable to Dryden Community Center Café, Inc.

And mail to: P.O. Box 801 Dryden, New York 13053
