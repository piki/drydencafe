Schedule of Community Open Houses

Thursday, March 25th

Ray and Rita Harris will be in our cafe to talk about the history of the Green Hills Cemetary that they currently maintain for our community. Stop by and learn more about this historic site and what you could do to help maintain this important part of our village's history!

Thursday, April 8th

Members of the community who operate and maintain the Green Hills Cemetery (located at the top of Highland Drive in the village of Dryden) will be at the Cafe from 7 to 8:30 PM to discuss the history of the Cemetery and to answer questions concerning the current operations and to provide information on the ways people can volunteer their time to assist in the maintenance of this historic Cemetary. Information will be on display at the Cafe beginning the week of March 29th and running through April 12th.

Thursday, April 22nd

Members of the local Habitat for Humanity organization (who recently opened an office in the village of Dryden) will be at the Cafe from 5:30 to 7:30 to discuss their upcoming project on Wellsley Dr. in Dryden as well as the Lansing project that is currently under construction. Information will also be available on the variety of ways individuals can volunteer their time to assist on the Wellsley Dr. project. A display will be at the Cafe beginning the week of April 12th and running through the end of April.
