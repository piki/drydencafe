Welcome New Board Members!

First we would like to thank our retiring board members, Monica Knight and Evan Kurtz for all of their hard work and dedication to the Cafe over the past two years. Both leave knowing that the cafe is running strong and are now going to focus on new challenges.

Monica Knight will be taking on Dryden Old Home Days, as well as continuing as our Music Coordinator. Evan Kurtz is now President of the Rotary Club.

New to the board are Stephanie McConnell and Bill Tyler. Stephanie has accepted the position of Treasurer. Her eye for financial detail will be a great asset to the cafe. Bill has been a longtime volunteer and is very knowledgeable about the daily activities and challenges the cafe faces.

Beth Peck is our new President, and Deb Fisher has taken on the position of Vice President. We would also like to thank Nancy Hart for her interest in updating the overall look of the cafe. She has a new team of volunteers looking to make our cafe and even friendlier place to sit and chat!
