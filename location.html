Location

The Café is located at 1 West Main Street in the center of Dryden, NY, at the intersection of Route 13, Route 38, and Route 392. There is parallel parking available along Main Street (Route 13) and South Street (Route 38) and free municipal parking behind the Café at the corner of South and George.

The Café is 10 miles from Ithaca and 10 miles from Cortland.
