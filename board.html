Board of Trustees Election

At the Café's first annual membership meeting on January 22nd, we will elect our first Board of Trustees to a two-year term. If you wish to vote, become a member before the 22nd, and attend the meeting, at 7pm in the Café. The meeting and election will occur in accordance with the proposed bylaws.

The nominating committee will choose a "recommended slate" of ten candidates to run for election. Additional candidates may be nominated (or may nominate themselves) at the meeting. The ten candidates receiving the most votes will serve as the Board of Trustees. One of the ten positions is reserved for a nonvoting youth candidate, between ages 13 and 18.

If you are interested in running for election, either contact the nominating committee chair, Patrick Reynolds, to be considered for the recommended slate, or attend the meeting on the 22nd and nominate yourself.
