Come Join the Fun: Be a Volunteer!

If you are looking for a place to make new friends or just spend time with old ones, here is the place for you. Volunteers are needed to help make sandwiches, pour coffee, participate with evening events, join in or lead a class, and just lend their time and energy to this great community project!

Download our Volunteer Opportunities Brochure and Form to review. Either send the completed form to:
